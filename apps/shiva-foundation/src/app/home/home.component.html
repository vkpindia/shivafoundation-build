ABOUT US

Our Team

Shiva Foundation have an enthusiastic and empathetic team willing to bring changes in life of people and smile on their faces . People from all avenues of life have joined hands to contribute for this noble cause of serving humanity by in various ways. Some are committed to actively participate as volunteers whereas some have chosen to help part time. Those who could not devote their time have also come forward to help financially and by other means.

Our History

Shiva Foundation was formed seeing a need for energetic,nonprofit work for old age home, providing them with basic amenities and a homely habitat. We've consistently grown since then, all thanks to the helping hands of this amazing community! Realizing the needs of poor children, we decided to extend this place to an orphanage as well because this brought more life to the place by connecting caring warm love of old people to the sheer innocence of children. We also provide education to the poor children which is a necessary step for the future of the tiny tots and our mighty nation.

Our Mission

We focus on making the maximum positive effort for Shiva Foundation family members as we call them. Our volunteers thrive to give a good life to people here by providing for food, and medical facilities to children and for old people along with nursing and care . We also support the children for their education and skills development to make them self dependent.
