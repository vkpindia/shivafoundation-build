ABOUT US

Message from Chairman

We founded Shiva Foundation with a vision to bring changes to life of people who are in need to warm nurturing, gentle care and a home. A society with happy senior citizens will be a healthy society with their guidance and blessings. There are many old age people unattended by their families left at this helpless stage to provide for themselves in a very sorry state. We must as a society feel responsible to make a difference to their life.

We decided to extend this to an orphanage also because along with physical needs,the idea helped us fill the gap of physiological need of the elderly people who thoroughly enjoyed the company of innocent homeless children and it also brought change to the life of children who could get good care and education here . This transformed our habitat into a home with happy family.

We invite everyone to contribute to this cause in any way possible. It helps in sleeping contended and make the world a happy place.
